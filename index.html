Trace!

What we did: Built an interactive, multi-player game that teaches supply chain executives "Blockchain 101" in ten minutes.

  • Players joins the game with 1-3 "products" (playing cards).
  • Each "node" (player) in the supply chain scans the product with a phone to reveal delivery instructions.
  • The nodes pass the products between eachother for 1-3 minutes.
  • At the end of the round, one of the nodes is revealed to be "contaminated."
  • The nodes must work together - inspecting their transaction records - to find, scan, and remove the products of contaminated origin.
